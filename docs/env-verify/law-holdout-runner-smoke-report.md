## Caveat

This is still an automatically generated smoke benchmark. The evaluation IDs are held out from training, but the
question style and answer style are generated by the same project machinery. The public claim should remain:

> The pipeline can measure and improve deterministic Korean law citation behavior under a closed-set protocol.

Do not phrase this as a final "small model beats large model" result until curated questions, larger law coverage,
and partial-span evaluation are rerun.
